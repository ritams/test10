Discover the Seven Wonders

Great Wall of China

Built over 2,000 years ago, the Great Wall of China stretches more than 13,000 miles across the northern border of ancient China. It was constructed to protect Chinese states against raids and invasions.

Taj Mahal

A magnificent white marble mausoleum in Agra, India, built by Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his beloved wife. The Taj Mahal is considered the finest example of Mughal architecture.

Colosseum

An oval amphitheater in the center of Rome, Italy, built of concrete and sand. It is the largest amphitheater ever built and was used for gladiatorial contests and public spectacles.

Christ the Redeemer

A massive statue of Jesus Christ atop Mount Corcovado in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil. Standing 98 feet tall, it has become an iconic symbol of both Rio de Janeiro and Brazil.

Petra

An ancient city carved from rose-colored rock in southern Jordan. Established possibly as early as 312 BC, it was the capital of the Nabataean Kingdom and features elaborate tombs and temples.

Machu Picchu

An Incan citadel set high in the Andes Mountains in Peru. Built in the 15th century and later abandoned, it's renowned for its sophisticated dry-stone walls and panoramic views.
